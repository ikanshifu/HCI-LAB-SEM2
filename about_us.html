OUR STORY

Our company was founded in the year 1908 by Will Keith Kellogg. We are one of the most well-known major cereal manufacturer and food product innovation company in the word. Started from a small store in the middle of Battle Creek, into a multi million company, recognized worldwide. We are committed to providing the best product innovations, recipes and service to all of our costumers around the world. Producing only the highest quality of products and building trust and loyalty within our costumer base.
